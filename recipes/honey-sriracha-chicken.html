Honey Sriracha Chicken

Description

This is a great recipe for people that enjoy the taste of sriracha as well as american chinese food such as General Tso's Chicken.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Sriracha
  • Honey
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Cook your chicken.
  2. Prepare your sauce by mixing honey and sriracha.
  3. Cook your rice.
  4. Combine all parts and enjoy!
