Pour Over Coffee

Description

Brewing your coffee using a hario v60 will result in extracting more satisfying, flavorful brews from your favorite local beans.

Ingredients

  • 30mg Coffee Beans
  • 1 V60 Filter Paper
  • 500mg Water

Steps

  1. Grind your beans and preheat your kettle.
  2. Wet the filter that you place into your v60 brewer to remove paper taste.
  3. Pour 500mg of water from your kettle over the ground beans.
